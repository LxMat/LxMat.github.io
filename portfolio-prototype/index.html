SpaceBeats

A virtual drumkit

SpaceBeats is a project that was developed multimodal interactions and interfaces at KTH. It is a virtual drumkit that senses arm movement and muscle tensions from the arm using the MYO armband. by moving the arm in one direction and flicking your finger, the selected sound is created. Besides myself, SpaceBeats was developed by: Hjalmar Jakobsson,Martin Lind Oros, Glen Schmitz
